I AM A DEDICATED GAME DESIGNER

ABOUTME

Hello, i'm Thomas a 24 years old.

I've got a bachelor Degree's in Animation and I'm currently studying at Gamesup School in Lyon in order to get a second bachelor in Game Design.

I started playing games when I was 4 years old and since I'm 11 my life goal has become to create them.

Video games, in my opinion are the best way to transmit emotions and messages and I believe that they can talk to other like they talked to me. I began to study game design in order to understand how to talk to people and transmit emotions through game mechanics

I hope I will one day make someone as happy as I was by playing Kingdom hearts games during my childhood.

My years of studies brought me lots of opportunity to improve myself as a future professional.

I'm actually looking for an internship in order to validate my bachelor.

On this Website you can discover the project's I worked on during my studies & in my spare time.

Hope you like them.

LONG STORY SHORT

You probably read this kind of sentence pretty often but :

My dream is to make you feel the best moment of your life through one of my games.

When I was child , playing video games was just a way to kill some time and discover new universes or stories but nothing very relevant.

But one day when I was 11 I played one game wich changed my life.

I defeated a boss after hours of trying , my heart was beating like crazy, and at this precise moment nothing else around me existed, only me the character and the boss.

This feeling , the stress entertwined with the excitation brought by the confrontation, the satisfaction granted by winning , and the marvelous cinematic after left me in awe.

Since that day , I don't know if I ever felt anything that strong, but I understood one thing.

I knew that I would have achieved something great in my life if I could make someone feel such a strong emotion with a game of my own.

Ever since, this is my life goal.
